Borang Reten MTL 25-26 Reporting Framework

Slide 30 of 31

District Health Office (PKD)

Responsible for district-level field operations, disease surveillance, & PPS wellness.

  • Borang 3: Deployed Medical, Health & MHPSS Teams
  • Borang 4: Affected Health Facilities Status (Clinics)
  • Borang 5.1/5.2: Health Education & Public Outreaches
  • Borang 6/7: Disease Surveillance & Outbreaks at PPS
  • Borang 8 & 11: Vector Control & Drinking Water Safety
  • Borang 13 & 14: Food Safety Inspections & MHPSS Screenings

Hospitals & Dental (PKPD)

Responsible for clinical admissions, emergency referrals, and clinical & dental staffing status.

  • Borang 12 (Daily & Cumulative): Admissions of flood victims referred from PPS or self-presenting (specifically tracking Antenatal, Postnatal, Haemodialysis, and Palliative patients).
  • Borang 15 (Human Resources): Daily staff attendance, tracking affected or displaced medical (Hospital) and dental (PKPD) personnel and emergency coverage status.
Reporting Timeline: PKD, PKPD, and Hospital compile data daily and submit to CPRC JKN Kedah by 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM for state-level consolidation, followed by daily transmission to CPRC Kebangsaan KKM by 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM.
