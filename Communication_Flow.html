3. Communication Rules & Milestones

1. Field, Hospital & Dental (PKPD) to District (PKD)

  • Medical teams, public health officers, and water safety inspectors collect raw data directly at clinics and evacuation centers (PPS) using standard Borang Reten templates.
  • The Bilik Gerakan Hospital Daerah compiles admission (Borang 12) and hospital staff status (Borang 15) metrics and submits them to the PKD Bilik Gerakan.
  • The Bilik Gerakan PKPD (Pergigian) compiles dental staff status and attendance metrics (Borang 15) and submits them to the PKD Bilik Gerakan.
  • All raw field, hospital, and dental data is consolidated at the district level by PKD.

2. District Coordination & Verification

  • The PKD verifies the correctness of all figures (ensuring zero discrepancies between disease counts, PPS numbers, and clinical admissions).
  • The PKD sits as a committee member in the PKOB Daerah (District Disaster Operations Control Center), where they feed validated health data and help verify coordinate reports from other field agencies (e.g. APM, Polis, JPS).
  • Consolidated district health files are sent from PKD Bilik Gerakan to the CPRC JKN Kedah by 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM to allow for state-wide analysis.

3. State to National (KKM)

  • The CPRC JKN Kedah aggregates data across all affected districts.
  • Under the authorization of the State Health Director, the consolidated reports are sent to CPRC Kebangsaan KKM by 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM daily.

4. Risk Communication

  • All press statements, media releases, and public health warnings must be coordinated through the Unit Komunikasi Korporat KKM and approved by the Pengerusi JKN (State Health Director) or Ketua Pengarah Kesihatan (DG of Health) to avoid conflicting information.
