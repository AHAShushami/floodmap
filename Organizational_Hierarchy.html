Committee Roles & Responsibilities

National (KKM HQ)

Coordinates federal policy, issues national guidelines, and controls strategic health resources and inter-state deployments.

  • Pengerusi: DG of Health
  • Naib Pengerusi: Timbalan KPK (Kesihatan Awam)
  • Urusetia: Bahagian Kawalan Penyakit (CPRC)
  • Key Members: Timbalan KPKs, Timbalan KSU, Pengarah Kanan (Farmasi / Pergigian / BKKM), Bahagian Directors.

State (JKN Kedah)

Coordinates preparedness and response across districts. Requisitions state logistics, handles inter-district movements, and updates the State JPBBN / PKOB Negeri.

  • Pengerusi: Pengarah Kesihatan Negeri
  • Naib Pengerusi: Timbalan Pengarah JKN (KA)
  • Urusetia: Ketua Penolong Pengarah (KPAS)
  • Key Members: TPKNs, Pengarah Hospital Utama, KPPs (Survelan / Vektor / NCD), Ketua Jurutera Negeri.

District (PKD & Hospital)

Ground execution level. Deploys medical/RRT/RAT teams, oversees evacuation centers (PPS) wellness, manages local hospital admissions, and collaborates with District PKOB.

  • Pengerusi: Pegawai Kesihatan Daerah (DHO)
  • Naib Pengerusi: Timbalan Pengarah Hospital Daerah
  • Urusetia: Pegawai / KP Kesihatan Persekitaran
  • Key Members: Pakar Kesihatan Awam, FMS, Pegawai Pergigian Daerah (PKPD), Farmasi, Pakar Kecemasan, PPKP (KPAS).
Sources: Garis Panduan Pengurusan Banjir KKM Edisi Ke-2 (2019) & Arahan MKN No. 20
